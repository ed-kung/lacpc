ITEM NO: 6  
TITLE: VTT-74890-CN-1A  
SUMMARY: A public hearing for a Vesting Tentative Tract Map to merge and re-subdivide a site into three ground lots with 353 residential and eight commercial condominiums. The project includes a mixed-use development with live-work units, office space, commercial uses, and subterranean parking. An appeal has been filed against the Deputy Advisory Agency’s Determination.  

------

ITEM NO: 7  
TITLE: CPC-2017-437-GPAJ-VZCJ-HD-VCU-MCUP-SPR  
SUMMARY: A public hearing for a project proposing new live-work units, office space, commercial uses, and a community room. The project includes a 36-story residential tower and an eight-story office building. Requested actions include certification of the EIR, a General Plan Amendment, a Vesting Zone and Height District Change, and various permits and reviews.  

------

ITEM NO: 8  
TITLE: DIR-2020-2067-TOC-1A  
SUMMARY: A public hearing for the demolition of a single-family structure and construction of a five-story multi-family residential building with 20 units, including two for Extremely Low Income Households. An appeal challenges the Planning Director’s Determination that the project is exempt from CEQA and the approval of density increases and incentives under the TOC program.
