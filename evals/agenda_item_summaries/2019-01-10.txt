ITEM NO: 6  
TITLE: ADM-2018-5752-DB-SIP  
SUMMARY: A public hearing is required for a proposed 6-story mixed-use development at 456 West 9th Street, San Pedro. The project includes 91 dwelling units, a community center, and commercial space, with 100% affordable units (excluding a market-rate manager’s unit). Requested actions include a Density Bonus Compliance Review and determination of the project as a Streamlined Infill Project exempt from CEQA.
