ITEM NO: 1  
TITLE: CPC-2020-1511-VCU-SPR  
SUMMARY: The agenda item involves the redevelopment of a 17.2-acre site for the Harvard-Westlake School, transforming it into an athletic and recreational facility. The project includes the removal of existing golf and tennis facilities to construct athletic fields, a gymnasium, a swimming pool, and tennis courts, along with parking and open space. The project requires several permits and approvals, including an Environmental Impact Report certification and a Vesting Conditional Use Permit.
