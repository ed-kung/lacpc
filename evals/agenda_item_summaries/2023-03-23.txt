ITEM NO: 6  
TITLE: DIR-2018-4190-TOC-SPR-1A  
SUMMARY: A public hearing for a proposed project at 3800-3830 North Pasadena Avenue and surrounding addresses. The project involves demolishing existing structures to construct a seven-story mixed-use building with 100 dwelling units, including 10 for Extremely Low Income Households, and 14,734 square feet of commercial space. Two appeals challenge the Director of Planning's determination regarding CEQA exemption and project approvals.
