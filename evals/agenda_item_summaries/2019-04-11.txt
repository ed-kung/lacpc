ITEM NO: 3  
TITLE: GENERAL PUBLIC COMMENT  
SUMMARY: The Commission will provide a 30-minute period for the public to address non-agenda items of interest within the Commission's jurisdiction. Speakers must submit a request form before the public comment period begins.  

------

ITEM NO: 4  
TITLE: RECONSIDERATIONS  
SUMMARY: The Commission may reconsider actions from previous meetings if a motion to reconsider is approved. This includes addressing the merits of any item the Commission has voted to reconsider, provided the Commission retains jurisdiction over the matter.  

------

ITEM NO: 5  
TITLE: CONSENT CALENDAR  
SUMMARY: Items on the Consent Calendar are considered non-controversial and will be enacted by one motion without separate discussion unless removed for individual consideration.
