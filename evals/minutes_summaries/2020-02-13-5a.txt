ITEM NO. 5a
CPC-2019-5273-CU

RELATED CASES:
CPC-2019-5273-CU, ENV-2019-5274-CE

SUMMARY OF AGENDA ITEM:
The agenda item concerns the proposed expansion of the Puente Charter School, a public charter elementary school located at 501 South Boyle Avenue. The expansion involves increasing the total enrollment from 167 to 360 students for grades Kindergarten through 5th Grade. The project will not change the building's footprint but will include interior tenant improvements, exterior structural upgrades, and the addition of two outdoor stairwells. The requested actions include an exemption from CEQA and a Conditional Use permit to allow the school's operation in the specified zone.

SUMMARY OF CPC DELIBERATIONS:
The deliberations were part of the consent calendar, indicating that the item was considered non-controversial and did not require extensive discussion. Commissioner Khorsand moved to approve the consent calendar, which included this agenda item, and Commissioner Perlman seconded the motion.

SUMMARY OF CPC MOTION:
The motion was to determine the project as exempt from CEQA, approve the Conditional Use permit for the school expansion, adopt the Conditions of Approval, and adopt the Findings.

ALIGNMENT OF MOTION TO PROPOSAL:
The CPC voted to approve the agenda item.

VOTE TOTAL:
9-0

RESULT:
MOTION PASSED
