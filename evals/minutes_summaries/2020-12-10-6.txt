ITEM NO. 6
CPC-2020-5811-CA

RELATED CASES:
CPC-2020-5811-CA, ENV-2020-5812-ND

SUMMARY OF AGENDA ITEM:
The agenda item involves a proposed ordinance to amend Section 12.03 and add Section 11.5.15 to Chapter I of the Los Angeles Municipal Code (LAMC). The ordinance aims to introduce definitions for "Private Detention Center" and "Community Detention Facility for Unaccompanied Minors" and prohibit these uses citywide in all zones. The ordinance also seeks to prevent any variance, exception, or deviation from this prohibition.

SUMMARY OF CPC DELIBERATIONS:
The City Planning Commission held a public hearing on November 12, 2020, to discuss the proposed ordinance. The deliberations included input from city planning staff and focused on the environmental impact and legal definitions related to the ordinance. The commission considered the administrative record and the Negative Declaration, concluding that the project would not significantly affect the environment.

SUMMARY OF CPC MOTION:
Commissioner Ambroz proposed a motion to approve the ordinance with modifications, if any, and recommend its adoption by the City Council. The motion included determinations regarding CEQA exemptions and the adoption of the Negative Declaration, as well as the adoption of the staff report and findings.

ALIGNMENT OF MOTION TO PROPOSAL:
The CPC voted to approve the agenda item and recommend its adoption by the City Council.

VOTE TOTAL:
8-0

RESULT:
MOTION PASSED
