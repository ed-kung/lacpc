ITEM NO. 8
CPC-2018-656-DB-SPR

RELATED CASES:
CPC-2018-656-DB-SPR, CPC-2018-617-DB-SPR

SUMMARY OF AGENDA ITEM:
The agenda item involves the demolition of a single-family dwelling and surface parking lot to construct a seven-story residential building with 95 dwelling units at 975-981 South Manhattan Place. The project will reserve eight units (11% of the base density) for Very Low Income Households and provide 149 residential parking spaces. The requested actions include determining CEQA exemption, approving a 35% Density Bonus, granting one On-Menu and one Off-Menu Incentive, and conducting a Site Plan Review for developments with more than 50 residential units.

SUMMARY OF CPC DELIBERATIONS:
The CPC deliberations included a public hearing completed on February 6, 2019, with attendance from city planning staff, the applicant, and representatives. The discussion focused on the proposed demolition and construction project, the inclusion of Very Low Income units, and the requested incentives and approvals.

SUMMARY OF CPC MOTION:
Commissioner Ambroz proposed a motion to approve the project with modifications, including determining CEQA exemption, approving the 35% Density Bonus, granting the requested incentives, conducting a Site Plan Review, adopting the modified Conditions of Approval, and adopting the Findings.

ALIGNMENT OF MOTION TO PROPOSAL:
The CPC voted to approve the agenda item.

VOTE TOTAL:
7-0

RESULT:
MOTION PASSED
