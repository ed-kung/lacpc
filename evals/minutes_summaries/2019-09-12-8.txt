ITEM NO. 8
VTT-74593-1A

RELATED CASES:
CPC-2016-3765-TDR-MCUP-CUX-ZAD-DD-SPR

SUMMARY OF AGENDA ITEM:
The agenda item concerns the merger and re-subdivision of three lots and the re-subdivision of a 0.38-acre vacant site located at 319 – 323 5th Street and 440 – 442 South Hill Street. The proposal aims to create two ground lots and four airspace lots. The item includes an appeal against the Advisory Agency’s determination, which reviewed and certified the Environmental Impact Report (EIR) for the 5th and Hill Project, adopted the Environmental Findings, the Statement of Overriding Considerations, and the Mitigation Monitoring Program, and approved the Vesting Tentative Tract Map No. 74593 with conditions.

SUMMARY OF CPC DELIBERATIONS:
During the CPC meeting, the public hearing was held with attendance from city planning staff, the applicant, and the appellant. The deliberations involved reviewing the 5th and Hill Project EIR, considering the Environmental Findings, and discussing the conditions of approval for the Vesting Tentative Tract Map.

SUMMARY OF CPC MOTION:
Commissioner Ambroz proposed a motion to approve the merger and re-subdivision project with conditions. The motion included certifying the EIR, adopting the Environmental Findings, the Statement of Overriding Considerations, and the Mitigation Monitoring Program, approving the Vesting Tentative Tract Map No. 74593, and adopting the Conditions of Approval and Findings.

ALIGNMENT OF MOTION TO PROPOSAL:
The CPC voted to approve the agenda item as proposed, with the conditions outlined in the motion.

VOTE TOTAL:
7-0

RESULT:
MOTION PASSED
