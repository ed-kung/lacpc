SUMMARY OF CPC DELIBERATIONS:
The City Planning Commission (CPC) held a public hearing via Zoom, with attendance from city planners, the applicant's representative, the appellant, and a representative from Council District 13. The CPC reviewed the Environmental Impact Report and considered the appeal against the Advisory Agency's decision.

SUMMARY OF CPC MOTION:
The motion proposed by Commission President Millman included certifying the EIR, adopting related findings and mitigation measures, denying the appeal, sustaining the Advisory Agency's decision, approving the Vesting Tentative Tract Map, and adopting the Conditions of Approval and Findings.

ALIGNMENT OF MOTION TO PROPOSAL:
The CPC voted to approve the agenda item, thereby denying the appeal and sustaining the Advisory Agency's decision.
