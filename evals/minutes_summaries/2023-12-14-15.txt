ITEM NO. 15
CPC-2022-5893-CU-DB-CDP-SPP-MEL-HCA

RELATED CASES:
CPC-2022-5893-CU-DB-CDP-SPP-MEL-HCA, ENV-2022-5894-CE

SUMMARY OF AGENDA ITEM:
The agenda item involves the after-the-fact demolition of a 6,952 square foot, two-story commercial building and the construction of a three-story, 13,095 square foot mixed-use building at 723 Ocean Front Walk in Venice. The proposed project includes 16 dwelling units, with three units designated for Very Low-Income Households, and 1,350 square feet of ground floor retail/take-out restaurant space. The project requests several actions, including a CEQA exemption, a Conditional Use Permit for increased density, a Density Bonus/Affordable Housing Incentive Compliance Review, and multiple waivers of development standards.

SUMMARY OF CPC DELIBERATIONS:
The CPC deliberations included a public hearing with attendance from city planners and the applicant's representative. The discussion focused on the proposed project's compliance with various planning and zoning requirements, including the requested exemptions, permits, and waivers.

SUMMARY OF CPC MOTION:
Commissioner Mack moved to approve the project as proposed, including the CEQA exemption, Conditional Use Permit, Density Bonus/Affordable Housing Incentive Compliance Review, Coastal Development Permit, Project Permit Compliance Review, and Mello Act Compliance Review. The motion also included adopting the Conditions of Approval and Findings.

ALIGNMENT OF MOTION TO PROPOSAL:
The CPC voted to approve the agenda item as proposed.

VOTE TOTAL:
7-0

RESULT:
MOTION PASSED
