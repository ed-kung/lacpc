ITEM NO. 6
CPC-2019-4968-DB-SPP-WDI

RELATED CASES:
CPC-2019-4968-DB-SPP-WDI, ENV-2019-4969-CE

SUMMARY OF AGENDA ITEM:
The agenda item concerns a proposed project at 15027 â€“ 15033 W. Ventura Boulevard, which involves the construction, use, and maintenance of a new six-story, approximately 30,870 square-foot mixed-use building. The building will include 33 residential units and 2,300 square feet of commercial space on the ground floor. The project proposes 49 parking spaces and 40 bike spaces, with vehicle access via two two-way driveways at the rear. The existing one-story commercial building will be demolished, and new landscaping, including 14 new trees, will be provided. The project requests several actions, including a CEQA exemption, a Density Bonus Compliance Review with incentives, a Project Permit Compliance Review, and a Waiver of Dedication & Improvement.

SUMMARY OF CPC DELIBERATIONS:
The CPC deliberations included a public hearing held via Zoom webinar/teleconference, with participation from city planning staff, representatives of the applicant, and a representative from the office of Councilmember David Ryu. The discussion focused on the proposed project details, requested actions, and compliance with relevant planning guidelines.

SUMMARY OF CPC MOTION:
The motion proposed by Commissioner Perlman included determining the project is exempt from CEQA, approving the Density Bonus Compliance Review with incentives, approving the Project Permit Compliance Review, denying the Waiver of Dedication & Improvement, adopting the Conditions of Approval, and adopting the Findings.

ALIGNMENT OF MOTION TO PROPOSAL:
The CPC voted to approve the agenda item with modifications, specifically denying the Waiver of Dedication & Improvement.

9-0

RESULT:
MOTION PASSED
