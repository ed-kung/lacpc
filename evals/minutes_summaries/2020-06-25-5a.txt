ITEM NO. 5a
CPC-2019-627-CU-ZAD-SPR

RELATED CASES:
CPC-2005-6118-VCU-SPR, CPC-2008-3537-CU, CPC-2015-2664-CU

SUMMARY OF AGENDA ITEM:
The agenda item concerns a proposed project for the expansion of bleacher seating at the Sierra Canyon School's Athletic Field, located at 11047 – 11055 North De Soto Avenue. The proposal seeks to increase seating from 300 to 1,630 seats, a net increase of 1,330 seats. The project includes a request for shared parking arrangements totaling 326 spaces and involves grading activities. No increase in school enrollment or impact on protected trees is proposed. The project is seeking several approvals, including a Conditional Use Permit, a Zoning Administrator’s Determination, and a Site Plan Review.

SUMMARY OF CPC DELIBERATIONS:
The City Planning Commission (CPC) discussed the proposed expansion of the bleacher seating and associated requests. The deliberations included input from city planners and the applicant's representative. The project was initially removed from the consent calendar for further discussion.

SUMMARY OF CPC MOTION:
Commissioner Ambroz proposed a motion to approve the project with modifications, including the expansion of bleacher seating, shared parking arrangements, and a Site Plan Review. The motion also included adopting the Conditions of Approval as modified by the Commission and adopting the Findings.

ALIGNMENT OF MOTION TO PROPOSAL:
The CPC voted to approve the agenda item as proposed, with modifications.

VOTE TOTAL:
8-0

RESULT:
MOTION PASSED
