ITEM NO. 5c
CPC-2024-2616-CA

RELATED CASES:
CPC-2024-2616-CA, ENV-2024-2617-CE

SUMMARY OF AGENDA ITEM:
The agenda item concerns the proposed Redevelopment Plan Procedures Chapter 1A Transition Ordinance, which aims to relocate the Redevelopment Plan Procedures from Section 11.5.14 in Chapter 1 of the Los Angeles Municipal Code (LAMC) to Division 13B.12 in Article 13 of Chapter 1A of the LAMC. The ordinance seeks to make these procedures available to projects within both existing and newly adopted community plan areas. The proposed amendments include policy changes to clarify relationships between regulations, standardization of procedures, and improvements in clarity and readability.

SUMMARY OF CPC DELIBERATIONS:
The deliberations involved a presentation by Tyler Currie, Nuri Cho, and Hagu Solomon-Cary from the Planning Department. The discussion focused on the relocation and amendment of the Redevelopment Plan Procedures to improve clarity, standardization, and policy alignment within the LAMC.

SUMMARY OF CPC MOTION:
Commissioner Cabildo moved to approve the actions related to the proposed ordinance, including determining its exemption from CEQA, recommending the City Council adopt the ordinance, adopting the Staff Recommendation Report, and adopting the findings. Commissioner Klein seconded the motion.

ALIGNMENT OF MOTION TO PROPOSAL:
The CPC voted to approve the agenda item.

VOTE TOTAL:
7-0

RESULT:
MOTION PASSED
