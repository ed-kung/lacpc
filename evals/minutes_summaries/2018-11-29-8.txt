ITEM NO. 8
CPC-2016-4345-CA

RELATED CASES:  
CPC-2016-4345-CA, ENV-2016-4346-CE

SUMMARY OF AGENDA ITEM:  
The agenda item involves a proposed ordinance to amend Sections 12.03 and 12.22 and repeal portions of Section 12.24 of Chapter 1 of the Los Angeles Municipal Code (LAMC). The purpose of these amendments is to regulate Accessory Dwelling Units (ADUs) in accordance with State law. The City Planning Commission is tasked with recommending actions to the City Council, including determining CEQA exemption status, approving the ordinance, adopting the staff report, and adopting the findings.

SUMMARY OF CPC DELIBERATIONS:  
The City Planning Commission discussed the proposed ordinance, with attendance from city planning staff and representatives from the offices of Councilmembers Koretz and Ryu. The deliberations focused on the ordinance's compliance with CEQA and its alignment with state law regarding ADUs.

SUMMARY OF CPC MOTION:  
Commissioner Dake Wilson proposed a motion to recommend that the City Council determine the ordinance is exempt from CEQA, approve the ordinance, adopt the staff report as the Commission's report, and adopt the findings. Commissioner Khorsand seconded the motion.

ALIGNMENT OF MOTION TO PROPOSAL:  
The CPC voted to approve the agenda item.

VOTE TOTAL:  
7-0

RESULT:  
MOTION PASSED
