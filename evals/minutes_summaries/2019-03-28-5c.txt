SUMMARY OF AGENDA ITEM:
The agenda item concerns a proposed project at 6847-6875 North Woodley Avenue, which involves the demolition of existing single-family and commercial buildings to construct a 140,000 square-foot, three-story self-storage building. The project includes 37 vehicle parking spaces and 28 bicycle parking spaces, with proposed operating hours from 6:00 a.m. to 10:00 p.m. daily. The requested actions include consideration of the Mitigated Negative Declaration, a Zone Change and Height District Change, a Building Line Removal, Conditional Use permits, and a Site Plan Review.

The agenda item was included in the Consent Calendar, indicating it was considered non-controversial. There was no separate discussion of this item during the meeting, as it was treated as part of a group of items to be enacted by one motion.
