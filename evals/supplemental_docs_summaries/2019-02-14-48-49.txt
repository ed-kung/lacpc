SUMMARY OF DOCUMENT:
The document is a letter from Doane Liu, the Executive Director of the Los Angeles Department of Convention and Tourism Development, addressed to the Los Angeles City Planning Commission. The letter expresses strong support for the Olympia Project at 1001 Olympic Boulevard. It highlights the project's potential to enhance Los Angeles as a convention and tourist destination by adding 1,000 hotel rooms and creating a signage district in the entertainment district. The letter also supports the project's proposed parking ratio and emphasizes the economic benefits and job creation associated with the project.
