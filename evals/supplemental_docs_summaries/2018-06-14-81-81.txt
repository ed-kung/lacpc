SUMMARY OF DOCUMENT:
The document is a letter from Monica Rodriguez, a Los Angeles City Councilwoman for the 7th District, addressed to David H. Ambroz, President of the City Planning Commission. The letter requests the continuation of Item No. 11, concerning a project at 9433 Sepulveda Boulevard and 15500-15508 Plummer Street, to the next City Planning Commission meeting on July 28, 2018. The request aims to provide community members with more time to prepare and submit comments and to offer geographic convenience for public testimony by holding the meeting in the Valley.
