TITLE: Reconsiderations  
SUMMARY: The Commission may reconsider actions taken on previous agenda items, including motions to reconsider and addressing the merits of any item approved for reconsideration.

ITEM NO: 5a  
TITLE: CPC-2017-5423-VZC-HD-CUB-CUX-SPR  
SUMMARY: The project involves constructing an 18-story hotel expansion at the Hilton Universal City Hotel, adding 395 guestrooms and other amenities. Actions include reviewing the Environmental Impact Report, a Vesting Zone Change, Conditional Use Permits for alcohol sales and entertainment, and a Site Plan Review.  

------

ITEM NO: 5b  
TITLE: CPC-2023-4890-VZC-CU  
SUMMARY: The project proposes converting a multiplex theater into a Tesla Delivery Hub and Service Center, including tenant improvements and parking lot modifications. Actions include reviewing a Mitigated Negative Declaration, a Vesting Zone Change, and a Conditional Use Permit for automotive use near residential areas.  

------

ITEM NO: 5c  
TITLE: CPC-2024-2616-CA  
SUMMARY: The ordinance relocates Redevelopment Plan Procedures within the Los Angeles Municipal Code, clarifying relationships with city codes and standardizing procedures. Actions include determining CEQA exemption, recommending a Zoning Code Amendment, and adopting findings.  

------

ITEM NO: 6  
TITLE: CPC-2023-5986-CA  
SUMMARY: The ordinance updates the Adaptive Reuse Ordinance to apply citywide, incentivizing building conversions to housing and streamlining approvals. The matter is continued to October 10, 2024.  

------

ITEM NO: 7  
TITLE: CPC-2022-8609-ZC-HD-SPE-SPP-CU-SPR  
SUMMARY: The project includes hotel site improvements and constructing a self-storage facility. Actions include reviewing a Mitigated Negative Declaration, Zone and Height District Changes, Specific Plan Exceptions, and Conditional Use Permits. The matter is continued to October 24, 2024.  

------

ITEM NO: 8  
TITLE: AA-2022-8610-PMLA-1A  
SUMMARY: The project involves subdividing one lot into two. An appeal challenges the Advisory Agency’s determination, including the adoption of a Mitigated Negative Declaration and approval of the parcel map. The matter is continued to October 24, 2024.

ITEM NO: 9

SUMMARY: The project involves merging and re-subdividing four lots into three and a Haul Route for soil export. Appeals challenge the Advisory Agency’s determination, including the certification of the Environmental Impact Report and approval of the tract map.

Actions include considering the Environmental Impact Report, annexation, General Plan Amendment, Zone Change, and establishment of a Sign District.

ITEM NO: 11
