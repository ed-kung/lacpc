ITEM NO: 1  
TITLE: CPC-2023-7407-CA  
SUMMARY: The agenda item discusses a proposed citywide ordinance that establishes a uniform Conditional Use Permit requirement for hotel development projects. It includes replacement housing requirements for the loss of dwelling units due to hotel developments and introduces a voluntary housing program to place unhoused individuals in vacant hotel rooms. The ordinance involves amendments to various chapters of the city's regulations, and the City Planning Commission is requested to recommend the City Council adopt the ordinance, align it with existing codes, and incorporate it into the New Zoning Code.
